Meet Our Chefs
Our culinary team is led by passionate chefs who bring authentic Filipino flavors to every dish. Each chef is handpicked for their creativity, skill, and love for tradition.
Chef Jane Santos

With 15 years of experience, Jane specializes in Filipino comfort food and is known for her modern take on classic adobo.

Chef Miguel Reyes

Miguel brings farm-to-table philosophy to TrayTayo. He loves experimenting with local produce and sustainable seafood.

Chef Liza Dela Cruz

Liza is a pastry expert who crafts the desserts that make every TrayTayo meal memorable. Her leche flan is a customer favorite.

Our chefs collaborate on every menu, blending tradition with innovation.
