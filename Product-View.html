PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Chicken Inasal is a famous Filipino grilled chicken dish marinated in a blend of vinegar, calamansi (Philippine lime), lemongrass, garlic, ginger, and spices. It is traditionally cooked over charcoal and basted with a flavorful annatto oil that gives it its distinct smoky aroma, savory taste, and vibrant golden color. Perfectly juicy and tender, itâ€™s a well-loved comfort food that pairs best with garlic rice and atchara (pickled papaya).

HOW TO USE

Best enjoyed freshly grilled and served hot. Simply heat on a grill, pan, or oven until warm, then serve with rice and dipping sauces such as soy sauce, calamansi, or vinegar with chili. It can also be shredded and used as a flavorful topping for rice bowls, wraps, or salads.

INGREDIENTS

Chicken, vinegar, calamansi, lemongrass, garlic, ginger, annatto oil, salt, sugar, soy sauce, ground pepper.
