A Note From the Founder

Jordi Rodriguez

Founder, Owner and Chief Designer

Tray Tayo was born in 2020 — during one of the most uncertain moments of our lives. What started as a simple passion for home-cooked meals slowly grew into something beautiful: a way to bring comfort, connection, and warmth to every household.

I grew up in a family where gatherings were always centered around food. Mas masarap kumain kapag sama-sama. That’s why every Tray Tayo dish is prepared with intention — from the ingredients we choose to the care we put into every tray.

Thank you for supporting our small business and allowing us to be part of your celebrations. It is my joy to prepare something special for you and the people you love.

— Jordi

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tray Tayo?

Tray Tayo is a home-style food tray service offering curated meals perfect for gatherings, celebrations, gifting, and potlucks.

Do you accept same-day orders?

Yes — we accept same-day orders depending on availability. However, we recommend ordering 1–2 days in advance.

Where are you located?

We operate in the Philippines and deliver within our designated service areas.

Do you customize trays?

Yes, customization is available depending on the request and ingredient availability.

How can I place an order?

You may order through our website or contact us on our official social media pages.
