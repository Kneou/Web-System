A note from the founder

John

Founder, Owner, and Chief Designer

John founded TrayTayo in 2023 with a mission to bring the warmth of Filipino family feasts to every home. Inspired by his grandmother's recipes, John believes food should be delicious, accessible, and bring people together.

Our Mission: To celebrate Filipino cuisine and culture by delivering authentic, home-style meals to your doorstep.

Our Values: Quality, community, and innovation. We support local farmers and chefs, use sustainable packaging, and constantly improve our menu.
